Privacy Policy - Life Calendar

Last updated: 2025-11-18

Life Calendar ("the extension") is designed with privacy as a core principle. This extension does not collect, transmit, store, or process any personal data on external servers.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. All data entered by the user (such as date of birth and optional weekly notes) is stored locally in the user's own browser via chrome.storage.sync.

No external databases, servers, or third-party APIs are used.

2. How Your Data Is Used

All data is used solely for the core functionality of the extension, including:

  • Calculating your week number
  • Displaying the life calendar grid
  • Saving your weekly notes locally

This data never leaves your browser.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to any third parties. There is zero server communication.

4. Third-Party Services

The extension does not use:

  • Analytics
  • Tracking tools
  • Advertising networks
  • External scripts
  • Remote code

No third-party services are integrated.

5. Data Security

Because all data stays on your device only, the security of your information is tied to your own browser and device security. Chrome's storage.sync follows Google's default encryption and sync policies.

6. Children's Privacy

Life Calendar does not collect any data from users of any age, including children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated in the future, changes will be posted on this page.
